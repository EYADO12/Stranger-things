Stranger things

Stranger Things" is a hit Netflix series, blending '80s nostalgia with supernatural suspense. Set in Hawkins, Indiana, it follows kids entangled in mysteries when their friend disappears. With a mix of government secrets, eerie creatures, and the Upside Down, the show offers thrilling adventures and a touch of heartfelt nostalgia
